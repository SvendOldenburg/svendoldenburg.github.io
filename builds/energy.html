Energy

A personal spending tracker built as an installable app. Quick entry from anywhere, receipt parsing via Telegram, and a full picture of where money goes -- by category, account, entity, and currency.

Screenshot coming

Vanilla JS / PWA / PocketBase / Telegram Bot / VPS

Private app at energy.svendoldenburg.com. Install to home screen on iOS or Android.

Open app →

What it tracks

  • Every spend: merchant, date, amount, currency, category, payment method
  • Three accounts: Lunar (personal DKK), Mercury (MFC USD), Wise (travel multi-currency)
  • Business vs personal split, plus entity tagging (personal / Cairn / MFC)
  • Recurring expenses with monthly and yearly cost totals
  • Multi-currency reporting in DKK, EUR, and USD -- converted at entry time
  • City and country per transaction for travel spending analysis

How it works

Four views in a bottom-nav PWA: quick add, transaction list with filters, monthly overview dashboard, and a recurring costs breakdown.

The backend is PocketBase on the same VPS that runs the training tracker -- the same Docker + Traefik stack, just a new subdomain. Exchange rates are fetched live from the ExchangeRate API and cached per session.

Phase 2 adds a Telegram bot: send a photo of a receipt and Claude Vision parses the merchant, amount, and category. Send a text like "50 DKK coffee at La Glace" and it does the same. A confirmation card comes back, one tap saves it.

What's next

Telegram receipt bot (Phase 2). Budget tracking -- set monthly targets per category, see progress in real time (Phase 3). And eventually, Lunar open banking integration to pull transactions automatically rather than entering them manually (Phase 4).
